To learn how to install the 'Network Policy Server' role, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then 'Server Manager'...
  • Once in Server Manager, select 'Roles' at the upper left corner of your screen.
  • Then, at the far right side of your screen, select 'Add Roles' to launch the Add Roles Wizard.
  • The first screen of the wizard asks you to verify if this server is properly configured.

  • Click 'Next'.
  • Select the 'Network Policy and Access Services' option.
  • Once the 'Network Policy and Access Services' option is selected, click 'Next >'.
  • This screen of the wizard gives a brief introduction to 'Network Policy and Access Services'.

  • Click 'Next >'.
  • On this screen, the wizard will ask which components of 'Network Policy and Access Services' you want to install. In this case, we want to install the first component, 'Network Policy Server'.
  • Once the 'Network Policy Server' role has been selected, click 'Next >'.
  • On the next and final screen of the wizard, the wizard will display all the options you chose.

  • Press 'Install'.
  • Wait while the Add Roles Wizard installs 'Network Policy and Access Services'.
  • View the installation results and then click the 'Close' button.
