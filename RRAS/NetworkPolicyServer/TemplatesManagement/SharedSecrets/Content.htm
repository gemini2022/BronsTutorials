To learn about Shared Secret templates, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then 'Network Policy Server'...
  • Once in Network Policy Server, expand 'Templates Management'.
  • Then select 'Shared Secrets'.
  • Once 'Shared Secrets' is selected, in the preview pane to the right will be a list of all the Shared Secret templates that are created on this server. There is no Shared Secret templates created at this time, but if there were, they would be listed here.
