To learn about RADIUS Client templates, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then 'Network Policy Server'...
  • Once in Network Policy Server, expand 'Templates Management'.
  • Then select 'RADIUS Clients'.
  • Once 'RADIUS Clients' is selected, in the preview pane to the right will be a list of all the RADIUS Client templates that are created on this server. There is no RADIUS Client templates created at this time, but if there were, they would be listed here.
