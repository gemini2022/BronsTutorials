To learn about error codes, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then 'Network Policy Server'...
  • Once in Network Policy Server, expand 'Network Access Protection'.
  • Then expand 'System Health Validators'.
  • And then expand 'Windows Security Health Validator'.
  • Then select 'Error Codes'.
  • Once 'Error Codes' is selected, in the preview pane to the right will be a list of all the error codes that define whether computers are considered compliant or noncompliant when a SHV or its associated System Health Agent returns an error.
