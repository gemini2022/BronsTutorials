To learn about Connection Request Policies, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then 'Network Policy Server'...
  • Once in Network Policy Server, expand 'Policies'.
  • Then select 'Connection Reqeust Policies'.
  • Once 'Connection Request Policies' is selected, in the preview pane to the right will be a list of all the Connection Request Policies configured on this server.
