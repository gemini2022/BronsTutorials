To learn how to disable 'Routing and Remote Access', proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then 'Routing and Remote Access'...
  • Once in Routing and Remote Acces, right-click the server node, which in this case is 'RRASSERVER (local)'.
  • Then select the option 'Disable Routing and Remote Access'.
  • Once you select the option 'Disable Routing and Remote Access', a dialogue box will appear warning you that by disabling this service, you will be removing the router and its configuration, and that if you decide to re-enable this service, you will have to reconfigure it.

  • Press 'Yes'.
  • Once you press 'Yes' on the dialogue box, a message will appear asking you to wait for the 'Routing and Remote Access' service to stop.
  • The 'Routing and Remote Access' service is now disabled.
