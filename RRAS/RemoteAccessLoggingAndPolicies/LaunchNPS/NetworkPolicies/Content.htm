Network policies allow you to designate who is authorized to connect to the network and the circumstances under which they can or cannot connect. To learn about network policies, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Network Policies' on the left side of your screen.
  • Once 'Network Policies' is selected, on the right side of the screen, will be all the network policies that have been created for this server. In this case, there are two network policies created. These policies are the default policies created when RRAS is installed.
