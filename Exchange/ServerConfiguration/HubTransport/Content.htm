To learn about Hub Transports, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then open the 'Exchange Management Console' by selecting the 'Exchange Management Console' icon.
  • Once the 'Exchange Management Console' is open, expand the node 'Microsoft Exchange On-Premise'.
  • Then expand 'Server Configuration'.
  • After 'Server Configuration' has been expanded, select the 'Hub Transport' node.
  • Once the 'Hub Transport' node is selected, you can then view and manage information and data related to Hub Transports.
