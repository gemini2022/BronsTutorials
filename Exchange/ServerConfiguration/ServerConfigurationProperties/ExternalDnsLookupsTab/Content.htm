To learn about the External DNS Lookups tab, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then open the 'Exchange Management Console' by selecting the 'Exchange Management Console' icon.
 
  • Once the 'Exchange Management Console' is open, expand the node 'Microsoft Exchange On-Premise'.
 
  • Then select 'Server Configuration'.
 
  • After 'Server Configuration' is selected, select the option 'Properties' found in the Actions pane located at the far right side of the Exchange Management Console window.
