To learn how to view details of the remote access ports, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then open the 'Exchange Management Console' by selecting the 'Exchange Management Console' icon.
  • Once the 'Exchange Management Console' is open, expand the node 'Microsoft Exchange On-Premise'.
  • Then select 'Organization Configuration'.
  • After 'Organization Configuration' is selected, select the 'Federation Trust' tab, which in this case is already selected by default.
  • Once the 'Federation Trust' tab is selected, in the preview pane below it will be a list of all the Federation Trusts that have been created,. There are no Federation Trusts created, at this time, but if there were, they would be listed here.
