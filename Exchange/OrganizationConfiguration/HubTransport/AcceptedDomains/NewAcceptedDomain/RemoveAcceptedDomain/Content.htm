To learn how to remove an Accepted Domain, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting an Accepted Domain. In this case we will select the new Accepted Domain we just created, 'AcceptedDomain'.
  • Then select the option 'Remove' found in the Actions pane located at the far right side of the Exchange Management Console window.
  • And then finally press 'Yes' to remove the selected accepted domain.
