To learn how to remove Managed Content Settings from a Managed Custom Folder, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by expanding a Managed Custom Folder that has Managed Content Settings applied to it. In this case we will expand the new Managed Custom Folder we just created, 'ManagedCustomFolder'.
  • Then select its Managed Content Settings.
  • Next, select the option 'Remove' found in the Actions pane located at the far right side of the Exchange Management Console window.
  • Once the 'Exchange Management Console' is open, expand the node 'Microsoft Exchange On-Premise'.
