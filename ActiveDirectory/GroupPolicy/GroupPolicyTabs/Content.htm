To learn how to view Group Policy tabs, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then select 'Group Policy Management'...
  • Once in 'Group Policy Management', select a Group Policy. In this case we're going to select the Group Policy we just created, 'Domain Wide Group Policy'. Once selected, look to the right of the 'Group Policy Management' window to view the tabs.
