To learn how to get to a user's properties, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then select 'Active Directory Users and Computers'...
  • Once in 'Active Directory Users and Computers', select 'View' on the top menu of the 'Active Directory Users and Computers' window.
  • Then select 'Advanced Features'. This will unhide any additional User properties that are normally hidden.
  • Then, expand your domain, in this case it's 'bronstutorials.local'.
  • Once you expand your domain, click on the 'Users' OU.
  • Once you click on the 'Users' OU, look to right side of the window. There you will see a list of all the users in the 'Users' OU. To see the properties of a user, right-click on any user of your choice, in this case we're going to right-click on the user we just created.
  • Then select 'Properties'.
