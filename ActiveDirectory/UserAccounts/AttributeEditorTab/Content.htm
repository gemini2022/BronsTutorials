On the 'Attribute Editor' tab, you can configure and update attributes of the user.
  • Here we will select the 'badPwdCount' attribute.
  • And then click on the 'Edit' button.
  • In the value field we are asked to enter in a value. Here in this case we will enter in the value of 5.

  • Then press 'OK'.
  • Press 'OK' when finished.
