On the 'General' tab, you can configure basic information about the contact.
  • As you can see, the first section of the 'General' tab has already been filled in. This is the information you filled in when you first created this contact.
  • Here you can add a brief description of the contact.
  • Here you can enter in the office the contact is in.
  • This next section allows for additional information about the contact. Here we can enter in the contact's phone information.
  • If we click on the 'Other...' button...
  • This will give us the option to add in additional phone numbers.

  • Once the phone information is entered, click on the 'Add' button.
  • Then click 'OK'.
  • Here the contact's email can be entered in like so.
  • And finally, if the contact has a web page, that information can be entered here.
  • Like the phone field, if the contact has multiple web pages, the administrator can add more by clicking the 'Other...' button...
  • Then adding additional web page information here.

  • Once the additional web page information has been entered, click the 'Add' button.
  • Then click 'OK'.
  • When finished, press the 'Ok' button.
