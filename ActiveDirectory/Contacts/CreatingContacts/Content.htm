To learn how to create a contact, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then select 'Active Directory Users and Computers'...
  • Once in 'Active Directory Users and Computers', expand your domain, in this case it's 'bronstutorials.local'.
  • Once you expand your domain, right-click on the 'Users' OU.
  • Then select 'New'...
  • Then 'Contact'.
  • Once you get to the new contact form, start by entering in the first name of the contact.
  • We could also include the contact's initials, but it's not necessary, but for this example we will include it.
  • And then the last name. Notice that the 'Full name' field gets populated from the previous fields.

 
  • Then finally, enter in a display name.
  • Press the 'OK' button to create the new contact.
