On the 'Attribute Editor' tab, you can configure and update attributes of the user.
  • Here we will select the 'displayName' attribute.
  • And then click on the 'Edit' button.
  • Here you will see the current value of the attribute.
  • To change the value, simply type in the value you want to chage it to in the text field. In this case we are chaging the value to 'Jane Doe Sales Contractor'.

  • When finished, press the 'OK' button.
  • Press 'OK' when finished.
