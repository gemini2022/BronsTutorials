On the 'Organization' tab, you can once again configure additional information about the contact.
  • Here you can enter in the contact's job title.
  • And then, enter in the department where the contact works in.
  • Then the company where the contact works at.
  • You can provide the contact's manager. This is useful if you want to know who this contact reports to. To configure this, first select the 'Change...' button.
  • And then click on the 'Object Types...' button...
  • In order to find any contact that is stored in 'Active Directory', you first need to select the 'Contacts' object type.
  • Once the 'Contacts' object type is selected, press the 'OK' button.
  • Then type in the name of the contact's manager.

  • When finished, press 'OK'.
  • The last field represents the people that report to this contact. In other words, the people listed in the 'Direct Reports' field have defined this contact as their manager. Therefore, this field is not editable.

  • Press 'OK' when finished.
