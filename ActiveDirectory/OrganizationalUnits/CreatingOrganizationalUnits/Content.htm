To learn how to create a new organizational unit, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then select 'Active Directory Users and Computers'...
  • Once in 'Active Directory Users and Computers', expand your domain, in this case it's 'bronstutorials.local'.
  • Once you expand your domain, right-click on your domain.
  • Then select 'New'...
  • Then 'Organizational Unit'.
  • Here is where you will name your new orgainizational unit. In this case, we are going to name it 'New York'.

  • Below the 'Name' input field, you'll notice the option 'Protect container from accidental deletion'. It is not that often that you will make changes to your OUs once they are created, so it is a good idea to keep this option on.

  • Press 'OK' to create your new OU.
  • Now that you have created a new OU, you can now create another OU inside this OU. To do this, simply right-click the OU, in this case the 'New York' OU.
  • And select 'New'...

 
  • Then 'Organizational Unit'.
  • Then name the new OU. In this case, we're going to name it 'Users'.

  • Then press 'OK'.
  • Now you have a new OU called 'New York', and inside the 'New York' OU, you have a child OU called 'Users'.
