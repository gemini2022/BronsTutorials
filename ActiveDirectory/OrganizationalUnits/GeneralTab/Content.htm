On the 'General' tab, you can enter basic information about the OU.
  • The first part of the 'General' tab allows you to enter a description for the OU.
  • Next, enter your street information.
  • Followed by your city.
  • Enter in your state or province.
  • And then the zip code or postal code.
  • Finally, you can enter your country or region by first selecting the dropdown button.
  • Then selecting your country or region.
  • When finished, press the 'Ok' button.
