To learn how to get to a computer account's properties, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then select 'Active Directory Users and Computers'...
  • Once in 'Active Directory Users and Computers', select 'View' on the top menu of the 'Active Directory Users and Computers' window.
  • Then select 'Advanced Features'. This will unhide any additional computer account properties that are normally hidden.
  • Then, expand your domain, in this case it's 'bronstutorials.local'.
  • Once you expand your domain, click on the 'Computers' OU.
  • Once you click on the 'Computers' OU, look to right side of the window. There you will see a list of all the computer accounts in the 'Computers' OU. To see the properties of a computer account, right-click on any computer account of your choice, in this case we're going to right-click on the 'WS3' computer account.
  • Then select 'Properties'.
