To learn how to join a computer to the domain, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by logging into a Windows client computer and then selecting 'Start' or the 'Start Orb' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • And then right-click 'Computer'.
  • And select 'Properties'.
  • Once in the properties of the computer, go to the far right of the window and click on 'Change settings'.
  • From here, you'll want to click on the 'Change...' button.
  • Then in the 'Member of' section, select the option 'Domain'.
  • Once you select the 'Domain' option, enter in your domain.

  • Then press 'OK'.
  • Here you will be asked to enter in the name and password of an account with permissions to join the computer to the domain. In this case, we're going to use the 'administrator' account.
  • Then type in the password.

  • Click 'OK' when finished.
  • You will then get a dialog welcoming you to the domain.

  • Press 'OK'.

 
  • After the 'welcome' dialog, you will receive another dialog informing you that in order for the changes to take effect, you'll need to restart your computer.

  • Press 'OK'.
  • Then press the 'Close' button on the 'System Properties' form.
  • And then finally, press the 'Restart Now' button to restart your computer.
