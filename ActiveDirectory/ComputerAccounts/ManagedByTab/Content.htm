On the 'Managed By' tab, you can specify an owner or contact person who is responsible for the management of this computer account. The information on this tab is linked to the specified user's account information. To learn how to configure this tab, proceed with the following steps...
  • First, press the 'Change...' button.
  • Once you press the 'Change...' button, a form will appear where you can enter in the name of the person that manages this computer account. In this case, we're going to enter in the 'administrator'.

  • Then press 'OK'.
  • After pressing 'OK' on the previous form, you may be presented with another form that indicates your search generated multiple results. If this is the case, select the object from the list that best matches the name you are looking for.

  • Then press 'OK'.
  • After pressing the 'OK' button on the previous form, the 'Managed By' tab will link all the information of the account you specified to the corresponding fields on the tab.

  • Press 'OK'.
