To learn how to create a group, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then select 'Active Directory Users and Computers'...
  • Once in 'Active Directory Users and Computers', expand your domain, in this case it's 'bronstutorials.local'.
  • Once you expand your domain, right-click on the 'Users' OU.
  • Then select 'New'...
  • Then 'Group'.
  • Once you get to the new group form, start by entering in the name of the group.
  • Here in this section, you can choose in what group scope you want this group to be in. In this case, we will leave the scope as the default, 'Global'.
  • This section allows you to choose between the two group types. In this case, we will leave the type as the default, 'Security'.

 
  • Press 'OK' to create the group.
