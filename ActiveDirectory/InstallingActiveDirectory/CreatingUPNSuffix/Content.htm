Now that the domain has been created, in this case, the domain has been named to 'bronstutorials.local'. Eventually the users of this domain will have an email address which will end in '.com', this could confuse them when they log in. Creating a UPN Suffix will allow the user's account login to end in '.com' rather than '.local'. To create a UPN Suffix, proceed with the following steps:
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then select 'Active Directory Domains and Trusts'...
  • To access the UPNs for this forest, right click on 'Active Directory Domains and Trusts' found at the top left side of your screen.
  • Then open the properties.
  • Add the new UPN suffix in the 'Alternative UPN suffixes' input field.

  • Then click on the 'Add' button.
  • Click on the 'OK' button to commit to the changes.
