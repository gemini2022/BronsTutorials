To remove 'Active Directory Domain Services' from this server, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then 'Server Manager'...
  • Once in Server Manager, select 'Roles' at the upper left corner of your screen.
  • Then, at the far right side of your screen, select 'Remove Roles' to launch the Remove Roles Wizard.
  • The first screen of the wizard tells you that Windows recommends you complete some steps before proceeding with removing this role. If you feel comfortable with moving on, then click 'Next'.
  • In this next screen, deselect the 'Active Directory Domain Services' option.
  • Then click ‘Next’.
  • To begin the removal process, click on the 'Remove' button.
  • After clicking the 'Remove' button on the previous screen, the 'Remove Roles Wizard' will then begin removing 'Active Directory Domain Services' from this computer.
  • View the removal results and then click the 'Close' button.
  • After clicking the 'Close' button on the previous screen, the 'Remove Roles Wizard' will then prompt you to restart the computer in order for the removal process to finish.

  • Click the 'Yes' button to restart the computer.
  • After the computer has rebooted, the wizard will resume, giving you the removal results.

  • Click the 'Close' button to finish.
