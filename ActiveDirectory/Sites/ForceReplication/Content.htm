You can use this procedure to force Active Directory replication to occur between two domain controllers on a one-time basis when you want changes to be replicated from the server that received the changes to a server in another site sooner than the site link schedule allows. To learn how to force a replication, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then select 'Active Directory Sites and Services'...
  • Once in 'Active Directory Sites and Services', expand 'Sites'.
  • Then expand the 'NewYork' site.
  • Then expand the 'Servers' container.
  • Once in the 'Servers' container, expand 'NYDC1'.
  • Then click on 'NTDS Settings'.
  • At the far right of your screen, right-click on a server. In this case we're going to right-click on the 'LONDC1' server which is located in the 'London' site.
  • Then select 'Replicate Now'.

 
  • Once you press 'Replicate Now', you will get a confirmation dialog telling you that the replication was sucessful. This is a fast way of testing the replication in you 'Active Directory' enviroment to ensure that it is working correctly.

  • Press the 'OK' button.
