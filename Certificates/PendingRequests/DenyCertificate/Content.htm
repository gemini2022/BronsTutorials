To learn how to deny a certificate, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then 'Certification Authority'...
  • Once in Certification Authority, expand the root node for the CA, which in this case is 'RootCA'.
  • Then select 'Pending Requests'.
  • Once 'Pending Requests' has been selected, look at the window to the right. There you may see a list of pending certificate requests. These certificate requests resides in the 'Pending Request' folder because they are waiting to be manually issued by an administrator.
  • However, the administrator has the option to deny the request. To do this, right-click on the certificate.
  • Then select 'All Tasks'.
  • And then finally, select 'Deny'.
