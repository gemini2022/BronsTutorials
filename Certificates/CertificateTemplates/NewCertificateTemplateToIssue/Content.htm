Before certificates can be issued by a certification authority, the certificate template must be added to a CA.  To learn how to do this, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then 'Certification Authority'...
  • Once in Certification Authority, expand the root node for the CA, which in this case is 'EnterpirseCA'.
  • Then right-click 'Certificate Templates'.
  • And select 'New'.
  • And then 'Certificate Template to Issue'.
  • After selecting the 'Certificate Template to Issue' option, a list of the preconfigured certificate templates will appear. You may have to scroll down to find the one you want.
  • In this case, the certificate template we are looking to add to the CA is the 'Key Recovery Agent' certificate template. Once the template is located, select it.
  • Then press 'OK'.

  • Once 'OK' is pressed, the certificate template will be added to the CA and ready for issuing.
