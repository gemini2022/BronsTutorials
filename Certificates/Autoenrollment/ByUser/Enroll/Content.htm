Many organizations will use Group Policy to automatically enroll users, computers, or services for certificates. With automatic enrollment, a user might see a reminder with a prompt to complete the enrollment. However, there might also be times when a user or administrator wants to verify that all pending automatic enrollment requests are processed. To learn how to do this, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by logging into a client computer. Then select 'Start' or the 'Start Orb' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then type 'certmgr.msc' in the search field.
  • Now click on 'certmgr'.
  • Once in certmgr, right-click on 'Certificates - Current User'.
  • And, select 'All Tasks'.
  • Then, select 'Automatically Enroll and Retrieve Certificates...'. This will launch the 'Certificate Enrollment' wizard.
  • Once the 'Certificate Enrollment' wizard is open, press 'Next' to begin the certificate enrollment process.
  • On this screen, press 'Enroll'.
  • Wait while the request is being processed.
  • Once the enrollment process has succeeded, press 'Finish'.
