To learn how to start, stop or pause your VPN server, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then 'Routing and Remote Access'...
  • Once in Routing and Remote Acces, right-click the server node, which in this case is 'VPNSERVER (local)'.
  • Then select the 'All Tasks'.
  • From here, you'll have the option to 'Start', 'Stop', 'Pause', 'Resume' or 'Restart' the VPN server service if needed.
