Scope options are applied to any clients that obtain a lease within that particular scope. Active scope option types always apply to all computers obtaining a lease in a given scope unless they are overridden by class or reserved client settings for the option type. To learn how to configure scope options, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then 'DHCP'...
  • Once in DHCP, expand your DHCP server, in this case the DHCP server is 'dc1.bronstutorials.local'.
  • Then expand 'IPv4'.
  • And then expand 'Scope'.
  • Once 'Scope' has been expanded, select 'Scope Options'.
  • After you have selected 'Scope Options', look to the far right of the DHCP console window. In this section, you will notice that the 'Server Options' are listed. All scopes will get the 'Server Options' settings.
