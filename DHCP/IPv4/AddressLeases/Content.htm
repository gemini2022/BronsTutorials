To learn about the Address Leases folder, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then 'DHCP'...
  • Once in DHCP, expand your DHCP server, in this case the DHCP server is 'dc1.bronstutorials.local'.
  • Then expand 'IPv4'.
  • And then expand 'Scope'.
  • Once 'Scope' has been expanded, select 'Address Leases'.
  • After you have selected 'Address Leases', look to the far right of the DHCP console window. This section shows you which IP addresses are currently being used by clients. If you ever need to know the MAC address of a client, you can find it here.
