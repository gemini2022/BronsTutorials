To learn how to manage authorized DHCP servers, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then 'DHCP'...
  • Once in DHCP, right-click 'DHCP' at the upper left corner of your screen.
  • Then select 'Manage authorized servers...'.
  • Here you can authorize and unauthorize DHCP servers in 'Active Directory'.

  • Click 'Close'.
