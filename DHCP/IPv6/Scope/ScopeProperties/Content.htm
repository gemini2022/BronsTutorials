Each scope has its own properties. To learn how to get to the properties of a scope, proceed with the following steps...
  • Begin first by selecting 'Start' at the bottom left corner of your screen.
  • Then go to 'Administrative Tools'...
  • Then 'DHCP'...
  • Once in DHCP, expand your DHCP server, in this case the DHCP server is 'dc1.bronstutorials.local'.
  • Then expand 'IPv6'.
  • Once 'IPv6 has been expanded, select the scope .
  • Then right-click it.
  • And select 'Properties'.
